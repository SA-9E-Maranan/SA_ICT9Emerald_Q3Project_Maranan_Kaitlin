2 Ways to Load your Beep Card

  • Through teller booths or ticket vending machines:
    1. Approach the teller booth/ticket vending machine.
    2. Select the road option.
    3. Follow the instructions on the screen.
  • Through Beep Card e-Load Stations:
    1. Visit any Beep Card e-Load Station.
    2. Place your card into the slot.
    3. Follow the instructions on the screen and wait for your updated balance.
